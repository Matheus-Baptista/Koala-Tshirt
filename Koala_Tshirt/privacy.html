Privacy Policy

Koala T-shirt is committed to protecting the privacy of its customers and staff. We collect and store personal data such as names, contact information, and purchase history only for business purposes, such as order processing, communication, and marketing of new collections.

All customer data is stored securely and is only accessible by authorized personnel. We do not sell, trade, or disclose your personal information to third parties unless required by law.

Staff data is handled with the same level of security and confidentiality. Koala T-shirt complies with all Australian privacy laws and updates its policy as legislation changes.

Customers can request access to their stored data or request removal by contacting us at contact@koalatshirt.com.au.
